# Product Direction Catalog

A list of technical directions around which [[organization|Alov Intelligence]]
plans both communities and open-source products. These are **intentions, not
released products**: as of 2026-06-26, none of this exists as a finished product.
Development is bottom-up and community-driven (see [[open-source-first]]).

## Directions

| Direction | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| **Forecasting** | Forecasting, including time series, demand, and similar tasks. |
| **LLMs** | Large language models. |
| **Computer vision** | Computer vision. |
| **Remote sensing** | Earth remote sensing products. |
| **Network planning & optimization** | Network planning and optimization. |
| **Supply and demand modeling** | Supply and demand modeling. |
| **Geospatial solutions** | Geospatial solutions. |
| **SDI** | Spatial Data Infrastructure. |
| **Geospatial ETL** | ETL for geodata. |
| **Drones, self-driving, navigation** | Drones, autonomous vehicles, navigation systems. |
| **Simulation, digital twins** | Simulations and digital twins. |

## Notes

- Several directions cluster around **geospatial/geodata** work (remote sensing,
  geospatial solutions, SDI, geospatial ETL, drones/navigation), which is likely a
  thematic cluster.
- The communities in the strategy (see [[community-model]]) are narrower than the
  product list: LLMs, computer vision, remote sensing, network optimization,
  supply/demand modeling, geospatial solutions. In other words, the product list
  is **broader** than the list of first communities.
- Prioritization of directions ("first products") remained an open question in
  the source and is not fixed here.
