# Inbound Marketing and Promotion

The third pillar of the [[organization|Alov Intelligence]] strategy. Inbound
marketing is a way to attract contributors, partners, and users through value
rather than direct selling. It is explicitly named in the mission as a mechanism
for promoting initiatives.

## Elements

- **Content creation.** High-quality technical content: articles, case studies,
  tutorials, success stories, and other material that attracts and engages
  potential contributors, partners, and users.
- **Community engagement.** Social media, online forums, and specialized platforms
  for continuous interaction with communities and promotion of their achievements.
- **Events and outreach.** Participation in local and international technology
  events, conferences, and academic forums to grow awareness and attract new
  talent and partnerships.
