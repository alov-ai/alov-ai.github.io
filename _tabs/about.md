**Alov Intelligence** is a community-driven, open-source initiative set out to
reshape R&D and applied research in Azerbaijan — built **bottom-up**, in the open.

## Why we exist

Great products and great engineers don't get handed down from the top. They grow
from a community that builds in public, learns by doing, and shares everything it
makes. We start from real needs rather than a roadmap imposed from above, and our
ultimate goal isn't a single company or product — it's a **self-sustaining
ecosystem** of talent and innovation.

## How we work

- **Community & skills** — technical communities, mentorship, and workshops, with
  skill growth gamified through achievements, badges, and learning paths.
- **Open-source products** — built in the open, iteratively, from the bottom up.
  Everything we develop is open and shared.
- **Inbound** — content, community engagement, and events that grow local talent
  and put Azerbaijan on the map for advanced technologies.
- **Partnerships** — universities, industry, government, and sponsors working
  together to sustain the ecosystem.

## Who it's for

Our people are not only citizens of Azerbaijan. We welcome the global Azerbaijani
diaspora in the broadest sense, and anyone who shares the mission, wherever they are.
