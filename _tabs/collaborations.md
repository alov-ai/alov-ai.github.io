Partnerships are one of the four pillars of **Alov Intelligence**: we work with
**universities, industry, government, and sponsors** to build and sustain an
open, bottom-up ecosystem for R&D in Azerbaijan. This page collects the
collaborations behind that work and the content they produce.

## Academic

### Baku State University — Center for Digital Technologies and Applied Research

Our first academic partner. The Center creates educational material and applied
research that we help host and promote, growing local talent in advanced
technologies.

**Cryptography course** — a free, one-semester course (15 lectures and 17
hands-on Jupyter practicals), authored by the Center and hosted here. This is
the first part, with a second part in preparation. Released under CC BY-NC-SA
4.0. Materials are in Azerbaijani; the course page is bilingual (Azerbaijani /
English).
