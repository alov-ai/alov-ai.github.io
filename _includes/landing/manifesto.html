Why we exist

Great products and great engineers don't get handed down from the top - they grow from a community that builds in the open. We start bottom-up: ideas and products emerge from real needs, not from a roadmap imposed from above.

Everything we make is open-source first. The goal isn't a single company or a single product - it's a self-sustaining ecosystem of talent and innovation, open to the global Azerbaijani diaspora and anyone who shares the mission, wherever they are.
